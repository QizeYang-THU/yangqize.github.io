I am a PhD student at Tsinghua University working on efficient AI systems. My research focuses on LLM training and inference systems, wafer-scale computing, communication optimization, and architecture-system co-design.

I am interested in understanding how workload characteristics, memory hierarchy, interconnect design, and scheduling decisions affect end-to-end system efficiency.
